# Code of Conduct

This is an **education-first** course. Its value comes from practicing engineers sharing real, hard-won knowledge — not from selling.

## For lecturers

- **No recruiting, no advertising.** Lecturers are strictly forbidden from using the course to recruit or to advertise their companies.
- **Keep introductions short.** Share only the experience that's relevant to the lecture you're giving.
- **No recruiting during the course.** Show your skills through merit and through commitment to giving every student the best possible education. (You're of course free to stay in touch with students after the course ends.)

## For everyone

Be generous with your knowledge, respectful of everyone's time, and focused on helping students build. We're here to bridge academia and industry — let's keep the room about learning.
